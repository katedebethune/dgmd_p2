Welcome to Judith's Kitchen!

Judith's Kitchen is a market and cafe in West Newton, MA dedicated to providing fresh, delicious prepared foods from the finest locally-sourced ingredients. We offer prepared foods, artisinal baked goods, and a whole range of delectable, wholesome dishes for you and your family.

Chef Judith Kalish

Judith Kalish, a local chef and West Newton resident, loves nothing more than preparing beautiful meals from fresh ingredients for friends and family.

A graduate of The New England Culinary Institute, she has been working in the Boston restaurant scene since the early 1990s. Judith worked as a cook at Hamersley’s Bistro for eight years and later served as the founding chef of Orleans in Somerville.

She has also served as manager of prepared foods for Whole Foods Market, and most recently as manager of prepared foods at Foodie’s Urban Market in the South End.
